--------------------------

GAME-STYLE PROGRESSION:
-----------------------
Players would "research" from left-to-right within each tier:
1. Start with early marks (left side)
2. Progress through developmental improvements (middle)
3. Unlock modern/advanced marks (right side)

Quad mounts are highlighted (Color 6) as "premium" or "advanced" variants
requiring additional research even after unlocking the base mark.
