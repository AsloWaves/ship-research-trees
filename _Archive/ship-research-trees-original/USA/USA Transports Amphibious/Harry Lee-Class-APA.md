**President Monroe Conversion** - Harry Lee-Class was a pre-WWII attack transport converted from passenger ship. One ship (USS Harry Lee, APA-10) converted 1940 from SS President Monroe. Displacement ~10,200 tons, length 522 feet, carried 1,400 troops. Served in Pacific and European theaters including North Africa, Sicily, Normandy, Philippines, and Okinawa. Decommissioned 1946.
