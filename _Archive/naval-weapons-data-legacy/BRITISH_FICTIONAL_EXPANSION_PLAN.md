## Turret Generation Strategy

For each gun, generate:
1. **Base Turret Types** (4 types): Single, Twin, Triple, Quad
2. **Tactical Variants** (3 categories):
   - **DP (Dual-Purpose)**: High-angle AA capable (85° elevation)
   - **SP (Surface-Purpose)**: Lighter, faster traverse (40° elevation)
   - **Open Mount**: Minimal armor, very light (destroyer/escort mounts)

### Turret Count Per Gun:
- **Heavy Guns (18"-12")**: 4 base + 4 DP + 4 SP = **12 turrets per gun**
- **Medium Guns (10"-6")**: 4 base + 4 DP + 4 SP + 4 Open = **16 turrets per gun**
