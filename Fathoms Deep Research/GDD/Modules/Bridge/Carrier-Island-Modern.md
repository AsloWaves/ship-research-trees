## Description

Massive integrated superstructure on modern supercarriers housing Flag Bridge, Navigation Bridge, Primary Flight Control, Carrier Air Traffic Control Center (CATCC), and extensive radar arrays including the distinctive SPS-48/49 and SPY-1D systems.

## Historical Notes

Modern carrier islands grew substantially larger than WWII equivalents to accommodate advanced electronics and command facilities. The Nimitz-class island is over 150 feet tall. The newest Ford-class carriers feature a redesigned, smaller island moved further aft to improve flight deck efficiency. The island houses enough electronics to require dedicated cooling systems - an island hit can blind the entire battle group.
