## Description

Fully enclosed bridge structure with armored protection. Features gyrocompass installation, voice tubes, and dedicated chart room. Improved crew comfort allows for sustained operations.

## Historical Notes

The enclosed bridge became standard after Jutland (1916) demonstrated the vulnerability of exposed positions. The addition of gyrocompasses eliminated magnetic deviation errors, significantly improving navigation accuracy.
