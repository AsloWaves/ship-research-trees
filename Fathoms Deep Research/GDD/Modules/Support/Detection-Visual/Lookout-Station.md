## Description

Dedicated observation positions with optical equipment (binoculars, telescopes). Trained lookouts scan assigned sectors for surface contacts, aircraft, periscopes, and torpedo wakes. The Mk 1 Eyeball remains effective even in the radar age.

## Upgrades

Can be improved by:
- **Crew Training**: +10% range per skill level
- **Better Optics**: +15% range (interwar+)
- **Elevated Position**: +20% range (crow's nest)

## Stacking

Multiple Lookout Stations provide:
- First station: Full effect
- Second station: +25% detection chance
- Third+: Diminishing returns

## Historical Notes

Despite radar, lookouts remained essential throughout WWII and beyond. Radar cannot see everything - periscopes, floating mines, survivors in the water, and small craft often require visual detection. Japanese lookouts were legendarily skilled, often detecting enemies before radar.

## Limitations

- Weather dependent
- Requires alert crew (fatigue matters)
- Cannot see over horizon
- Night capability very limited
