## Overview

Main guns are the primary surface armament of warships, ranging from destroyer-caliber 4" guns to the massive 18.1" weapons of the Yamato class. This index catalogs all main battery weapons available in Fathoms Deep, organized by nation and caliber.

> **Module System**: Main guns are typically fixed to specific ship classes but can be upgraded within their caliber range. Barrel wear, fire control upgrades, and ammunition selection affect performance.

## Game Mechanics

### Gun Statistics

| Stat | Description | Affects |
|------|-------------|---------|
| **Caliber** | Shell diameter | Damage, penetration, module slots |
| **Barrel Length** | L/XX designation | Muzzle velocity, range |
| **Rate of Fire** | Rounds per minute | DPS, sustained damage |
| **Range** | Maximum firing distance | Engagement options |
| **Shell Weight** | Projectile mass | Damage, arc trajectory |
| **Turret Traverse** | Degrees per second | Target tracking |

### Ammunition Types
Each main gun can fire multiple ammunition types:
- **AP (Armor Piercing)** - Max penetration, citadel damage
- **HE (High Explosive)** - Fire chance, module damage
- **SAP (Semi-AP)** - Balanced penetration and damage

See [[_Ammunition-Index]] for detailed shell specifications.
