## Overview

This index catalogs all carrier-based and floatplane aircraft available in Fathoms Deep. Aircraft provide reconnaissance, strike capability, and air defense for naval forces. Each aircraft type has specific roles, performance characteristics, and armament options.

> **Design Philosophy**: Aircraft are force multipliers that extend a ship's reach far beyond gun range. Carrier operations require careful management of deck cycles, fuel, and ordnance.
