## ğŸš€ System Breakdown: Carrier Operations

### Carrier Mechanics
**Status**: ğŸ“‹ PLANNED (Phase 3A)

### Aircraft Types
1. **Fighters**: Air superiority, intercept bombers
2. **Dive Bombers**: Anti-ship, high accuracy

### Carrier Gameplay Loop

2. **Control Squadron**: RTS-style click-to-move
3. **Attack Run**: Manual attack command or auto-attack
4. **Return to Carrier**: Land and rearm/repair

### Aircraft Mechanics
- **Squadron Control**: 3-6 planes per squadron
- **Plane HP**: Vulnerable to AA fire
- **Ammunition**: Limited bombs/torpedoes
- **Fuel**: Return before empty or crash
- **Spotting**: Reveal enemy positions to fleet

### Carrier Balance
- **Vulnerable**: Carriers weak in direct combat
- **High Impact**: Aircraft devastating if protected
- **Teamwork Required**: Need escort to survive
- **Skill-Based**: Manual squadron control = high skill ceiling

## ğŸ”± System Breakdown: Submarine Warfare

### Submarine Mechanics
**Status**: ğŸ“‹ PLANNED (Phase 3A)

### Depth System
- **Surface** (0m): Visible, can fire deck guns
- **Periscope Depth** (-10m): Limited visibility, can use periscope
- **Shallow Submersion** (-50m): Invisible, slow, acoustics
- **Deep Dive** (-100m+): Safe from depth charges, very slow

### Submarine Weapons
1. **Torpedoes**: Primary anti-ship weapon
2. **Deck Gun**: Surface combat only
3. **Mines**: Defensive area denial (advanced)

### ASW (Anti-Submarine Warfare)
**For Surface Ships**:
- **Sonar**: Detect submarines (ping mechanic)
- **Depth Charges**: Anti-submarine weapon
- **Hedgehog**: Forward-firing ASW mortars (high-tier)
- **Aircraft**: Spotter planes detect subs

**For Submarines**:
- **Stealth**: Remain undetected
- **Acoustic Signature**: Movement speed = detection risk
- **Silent Running**: Slow speed, hard to detect
- **Evasion**: Dive deep, change course

### Submarine Balance
- **Stealth Advantage**: Hard to detect when submerged
- **Fragile**: Low HP, sinks quickly if caught
- **Limited Visibility**: Can't see much underwater
- **Teamwork**: Requires patience and positioning

## ğŸŒ System Breakdown: Zone System

### Tier 1-10 Zones
**Status**: ğŸ“‹ PLANNED (Phase 3B)

### Zone Characteristics

#### Tier 1-2: Safe Waters (Tutorial/Starting Zones)
- **Risk**: Minimal (0% ship loss, 0% crew death)
- **Rewards**: Low (basic loot, low credits)
- **NPCs**: Easy (patrol boats, light merchants)
- **PvP**: Restricted (safe zone mechanics)
- **Environment**: Calm, good visibility

#### Tier 3-4: Contested Waters
- **Risk**: Low (0% ship loss, 10-20% crew casualties)
- **Rewards**: Moderate (better loot, decent credits)
- **NPCs**: Moderate (destroyers, light cruisers)
- **PvP**: Allowed (no penalties for fighting)
- **Environment**: Occasional weather

#### Tier 5: The Threshold
- **Risk**: Moderate (0% ship loss, 30% crew casualties)
- **Rewards**: Good (quality loot, good credits)
- **NPCs**: Challenging (heavy cruisers, small fleets)
- **PvP**: Encouraged (loot incentives)
- **Environment**: Frequent weather

#### Tier 6-7: Dangerous Waters
- **Risk**: High (30-40% ship loss, 40-50% crew casualties)
- **Rewards**: Excellent (rare loot, high credits)
- **NPCs**: Difficult (battleships, carrier groups)
- **PvP**: Intense (high-value targets)
- **Environment**: Harsh weather, low visibility

#### Tier 8-9: Death Waters
- **Risk**: Extreme (50-60% ship loss, 50-60% crew casualties)
- **Rewards**: Exceptional (legendary loot, massive credits)
- **NPCs**: Elite (super-battleships, task forces)
- **PvP**: Ruthless (no mercy, high stakes)
- **Environment**: Storms, night combat

#### Tier 10: The Abyss
- **Risk**: ABSOLUTE (100% ship & crew permadeath)
- **Rewards**: Unmatched (unique items, enormous credits)
- **NPCs**: Nightmare (super-units, fleet battles)
- **PvP**: Total War (everyone vs everyone)
- **Environment**: Apocalyptic (hurricanes, darkness)

### Zone Transitions
- **Visible Boundaries**: Clear zone markings
- **Warning System**: UI alerts when entering higher tier
- **Gradual Escalation**: Zones arranged geographically
- **Strategic Positioning**: High-tier zones in center of map

- Module damage increases (10% â†’ 30%)

#### Tier 6-7: DANGEROUS (30-40% Ship Loss)

- Module damage severe (50-70%)

#### Tier 8-9: EXTREME (50-60% Ship Loss)
- High ship loss probability
- Module damage catastrophic (70-90%)

- **GUARANTEED PERMADEATH**
- Ship lost forever
- All crew dead
- All cargo lost
- No recovery possible

### Crew Permadeath (Per Tier)

**Casualty Rates**:
- Tier 1-4: 0-20% per battle
- Tier 5: 30% per battle
- Tier 6-7: 40-50% per battle
- Tier 8-9: 50-60% per battle
- Tier 10: 100% (all crew die)

**Crew Revival**:
- Dead crew = PERMANENTLY DEAD
- No resurrection mechanics
- Must recruit replacements
- Lose all crew progression

### Cargo Permadeath (Always)
- **Unextracted Loot**: ALWAYS lost on death
- **Secured Loot**: Safe in port inventory
- **No Exceptions**: Applies to all tiers

- **Clear Warnings**: Zone entry shows permadeath risk
