Advanced ASW (OQQ-22 sonar, OQR-2 towed array). SH-60K helicopter. COGLAG hybrid propulsion (gas turbine + electric motors). Crew 200 (highly automated). Multi-role: Air defense primary, also ASW and anti-surface. Cost-effective air defense without Aegis expense. All 4 active as of 2025. Demonstrate Japanese radar technology leadership - FCS-3A rivals foreign systems.

**FCS-3A AESA Radar - 4 Arrays:**

**Revolutionary Technology:**
- **First Japanese indigenous AESA for warships**
- Shows Japan's advanced radar capabilities
- Rivals foreign systems (Dutch APAR, US SPY-6)

**How It Works:**
- **Active Electronically Scanned Array (AESA)**
- X-band (high frequency, high resolution)
- Each array: Many transmit/receive modules
- Electronic beam steering (no moving parts)
- Simultaneous search and track

**4-Array Configuration:**
- Forward array: 090-180° coverage (centered forward)
- Starboard array: 000-090° coverage
- Aft array: 180-270° coverage
- Port array: 270-360° coverage
- **Complete 360° coverage** (like Aegis layout)

**Capabilities:**
- Track dozens of targets simultaneously (exact number classified, likely 30-50)
- Guide ESSM missiles (multiple engagements)
- Surface search (detect ships, small craft)
- Fire control (target tracking for guns)
- Air search (integrated with OPS-50)
- Low-altitude tracking (sea-skimming missiles)

**Vs Aegis:**
- Aegis SPY-1: Passive S-band (receive only) + separate illuminators
- FCS-3A: Active X-band (transmit and receive, no separate illuminators needed)
- Different technology, similar concept (360° coverage, multi-target)
- FCS-3A less capable than Aegis (smaller arrays, fewer targets) but much less expensive

- Range: 50+ km (27+ nm)

**COGLAG Propulsion:**

**Combined Gas turbine eLectric And Gas turbine:**
- 4× gas turbines (total power)
- Electric motors (for cruise)
- 2 shafts
- Total: 69,000 shp

**How It Works:**
- **Cruise (low speed):** Electric motors only (quiet, efficient)
- **Transit (medium speed):** Gas turbines alone
- **Sprint (high speed):** Gas turbines + electric motors (maximum power)

**Advantages:**
1. **Fuel efficiency:** Electric cruise saves fuel
2. **Quieter:** Electric quieter than gas turbines (ASW benefit)
3. **Flexibility:** Multiple power modes
4. **Range:** Better range than pure gas turbine (COGAG)

**Comparison:**
- Kongo/Atago: COGAG (gas turbines only)
- Maya: COGLAG (hybrid)
- **Akizuki: COGLAG** (early adopter of hybrid)

**Result:**
- 30 knots max speed (adequate)
- 4,500 nm range (good)
- Quiet at low speeds (ASW operations)
