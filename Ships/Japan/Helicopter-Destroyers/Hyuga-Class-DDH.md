**First Full-Length Flight Deck** - Hyuga-class were revolutionary Japanese helicopter destroyers. 2 ships commissioned 2009, 2011. Large (19,000 tons - largest Japanese ships at time). Full-length flight deck (646 ft) - carrier-like appearance. Island superstructure starboard side. Two aircraft elevators. Up to 11 helicopters (SH-60K, MCH-101). 16 VLS cells (ESSM), SeaRAM, 2 Phalanx. Advanced ASW (OQQ-22 sonar, towed array). 30 knots, COGAG propulsion. Primary mission: Anti-submarine warfare. Secondary: Command ship, disaster relief. Controversial design (resembles carriers) but officially "destroyers." Both active as of 2025. Pioneered full-deck design that evolved into Izumo-class. Bridge between conventional destroyers and light carriers.

**Key Specs:** 13,950/19,000 tons | 646ft | 30 kn | 11 helicopters | 16× VLS (ESSM) | OPS-50 AESA | OQQ-22 sonar | 2 ships active

**Innovation:** Revolutionary design for JMSDF (2009, 2011). First full-length flight deck - no forward VLS/guns blocking deck (compare Shirane: helicopter deck aft only). Carrier-like layout: Island superstructure starboard side, flight deck port side clear. Two aircraft elevators (forward and aft of island). Full-length hangar below deck. Accommodation for 11 helicopters (SH-60K ASW, MCH-101 mine countermeasures). Largest Japanese warships when commissioned (19,000 tons - later surpassed by Izumo 27,000). Advanced ASW: OQQ-22 active/passive sonar, OQR-2 towed array, SH-60K helicopters with dipping sonar/torpedoes. 16-cell Mk 41 VLS forward (ESSM quad-pack, 64 missiles). SeaRAM (rolling airframe missiles). 2× Phalanx CIWS. FCS-3A Japanese AESA radar. OPS-50 3D AESA. COGAG propulsion (4× LM2500, 100,000 shp, 30 knots). Multi-role: ASW primary, command ship, disaster relief, amphibious support. Tested full-deck concept for later Izumo-class.

**Design:** Large helicopter destroyer hull (646 ft, 19,000 tons). Full-length flight deck (carrier appearance - politically sensitive). Island superstructure starboard side (bridge, mast, sensors, funnel). Flight deck: 5 helicopter spots (simultaneous operations). Two aircraft elevators (one forward of island, one aft). OQQ-22 hull sonar (active/passive ASW). OQR-2 TACTASS towed array. FCS-3A AESA fire control radar. OPS-50 3D AESA radar (air search). COGAG propulsion (4 gas turbines, 30 knots).
