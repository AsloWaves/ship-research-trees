**Innovation:** Early protected cruisers from Victorian era. British-designed and built (Armstrong Whitworth). Small by later standards (2,657 tons). Protected cruiser concept - 2-inch armored deck, no belt. Two 6-inch main guns. Six 4.7-inch secondary battery. Coal-fired horizontal compound engines (reciprocating). 20 knots (adequate for 1896).
