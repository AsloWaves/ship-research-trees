**Last Pre-Dreadnoughts** - Final German pre-dreadnought class. SMS Pommern only German battleship sunk at Jutland (1916). Schlesien and Schleswig-Holstein served until WWII, scrapped 1960s.

**Key Features:** Last pre-dreadnoughts | Pommern sunk at Jutland | Two ships survived until WWII

**Ships:** SMS Deutschland, Hannover, Pommern (sunk Jutland 1916), Schlesien, Schleswig-Holstein

**Service:** Jutland 1916. Schleswig-Holstein fired first shots of WWII (Westerplatte, 1939). Scrapped 1922-1960s.
