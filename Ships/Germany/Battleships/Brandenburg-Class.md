**First Modern German Battleships** - Germany's entry into modern battleship competition. 6× 28cm guns in unusual 3-turret arrangement (fore, aft, amidships). Heavy Harvey armor (400mm belt). Foundation of High Seas Fleet.

**Key Features:** First ocean-going German battleships | 400mm Harvey armor belt | Unusual amidships turret | Good seakeeping

**Ships:** SMS Brandenburg, SMS Wörth, SMS Weißenburg, SMS Kurfürst Friedrich Wilhelm

**Service:** Served in High Seas Fleet until WWI. Training ships 1910s. Scrapped 1919-1921 under Treaty of Versailles.
