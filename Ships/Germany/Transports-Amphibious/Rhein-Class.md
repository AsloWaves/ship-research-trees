**FAC Tenders** - Rhein-class (Type 401) were fast attack craft tenders for Bundesmarine. 8 ships 1961-1992.

**Key Specs:** ~1,500 tons | ~63m | 16 kn | ~40 crew

**Innovation:** Specialized tender design combining fuel/ammunition storage with workshop and medical facilities. First dedicated FAC support vessels for new Schnellboot fleet after WWII rearmament.

**Design:** Eight ships named after German rivers. Provided comprehensive support for 3rd Schnellboot Squadron: fuel, water, provisions, spare parts, ammunition, workshops, infirmary.

**Service:** Cold War Bundesmarine 1961-1992. Supported fast attack craft operations throughout period. Post-Cold War disposition: four scrapped, three to Turkey, one to Greece.
