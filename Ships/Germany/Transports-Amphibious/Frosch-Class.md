**East German LSTs** - Frosch-class were Landing Ship Tanks of East German Volksmarine. 12 built 1976-1993 at VEB Peenewerft, Wolgast.

**Key Specs:** 1,950 tons | 98m × 11.1m × 2.8m draft | 2× diesels, 5,000hp | 12 tanks OR 600t cargo + 100 troops

**Innovation:** East Germany's indigenous LST design (Project 108). Built by VEB Peenewerft, Wolgast. Hull numbers 531-542 (12 ships). Could carry 12 tanks OR 600 tons cargo plus 100 troops. Shallow 2.8m draft for beach landings.

**Design:** Simple bow ramp LST design. 2× diesel engines, 5,000hp total. Cargo deck for tanks/vehicles. Basic armament (details limited).

**Service:** Volksmarine service 1976-1993. After German reunification 1990, entire class not integrated into Bundesmarine. All 12 ships sold to Indonesia 1993 where they continued service with Indonesian Navy.
