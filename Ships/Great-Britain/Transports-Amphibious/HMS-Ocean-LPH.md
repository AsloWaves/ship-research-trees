**Helicopter Assault Ship** - HMS Ocean was Royal Navy's only dedicated helicopter carrier 1998-2018. 18 helicopter capacity. Deployed 480-830 Royal Marines. Fleet Flagship 2015-2018. Sold to Brazil.

**Key Specs:** 21,500 tons | 667ft | 18 kn | 18 helicopters | 480-830 Royal Marines | 170m flight deck

**Innovation:** Royal Navy's first purpose-built landing platform helicopter. 170m flight deck with 2 aircraft lifts. Could embark 12 Merlin + 6 Lynx helicopters or 4-6 Apache attack helicopters. First British ship to send Apache helicopters directly into combat action (Libya 2011). Commanded US Task Force 50 in 2016 - first Royal Navy vessel to lead this American formation.

**Service:** Commissioned 30 September 1998 at HMNB Devonport. First deployment: Hurricane Mitch relief (Honduras/Nicaragua). Major operations: Sierra Leone 2000 (Operation Palliser), Iraq War 2003 (Operation Telic - fleet flagship, awarded battle honour "Al Faw 2003"), Libya 2011 (first combat deployment of Apache from RN ship), London Olympics 2012 security. Fleet Flagship June 2015-March 2018.

**Notable Operations:**
- **2000 Sierra Leone:** Operation Palliser, deployed 300 Royal Marines, suppressed rebel activity
- **2003 Iraq War:** Flagship of RN task force, carried 300 Royal Marines + 400 air crew, 22 helicopters, 200 RN medical staff treated casualties both sides
- **2011 Libya:** Deployed with Apache attack helicopters alongside French Tonnerre (L9014) - historic first for RN Apache operations from ship
- **2016:** Commanded U.S. Task Force 50 during Joint Expeditionary Force (Maritime) deployment

**Decommissioning and Sale:** Ceremony 27 March 2018 attended by Queen Elizabeth II at Plymouth. Sold to Brazil for £84.3 million. Commissioned into Brazilian Navy 29 June 2018 as NAM Atlântico (A140), serving as Brazilian Navy flagship.

**Capabilities:** Full Royal Marines Commando Group deployment from 3 Commando Brigade. Amphibious assault and power projection. Humanitarian assistance and disaster relief. Medical treatment facilities. Command and control platform for task force operations.
