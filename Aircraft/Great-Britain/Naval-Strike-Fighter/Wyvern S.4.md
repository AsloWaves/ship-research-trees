## Notes

Turboprop strike fighter. Armstrong Siddeley Python engine (3560hp + 1100lb thrust). 13ft contra-rotating propellers. 10 year development (1946-1956). Multiple prototypes with different engines (R-R Eagle, Python, Clyde). First flight Dec 16, 1946. Plagued by propeller bearing failures, handling issues. Obsolete by service entry. 68 accidents, 39 lost, 13 fatalities. Withdrawn 1958.
