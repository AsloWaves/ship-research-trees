## Notes

Failed Barracuda replacement. Bristol Centaurus engine (2600hp). Mid-wing monoplane, internal weapons bay, retractable radar. First flight Jul 1945 - obsolete immediately. Designed for cancelled Malta-class carriers. Test pilot Eric Brown found controls "very heavy", barely movable ailerons. Programme cancelled. 7 ordered, 5 built, 4 flew.
