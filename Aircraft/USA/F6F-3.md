## Notes

Dominant US Navy fighter of WWII! 2000hp Pratt & Whitney R-2800 Double Wasp. Credited with 5,223 aerial victories - more than any Allied naval aircraft. Combat debut Sept 1943. Superior to F4F in all aspects. Excellent visibility, firepower, durability. Used at Philippine Sea, Leyte Gulf. F6F-5 night fighter variant had radar. Pilots nicknamed it "aluminum tank". 19:1 kill ratio vs Japanese.
