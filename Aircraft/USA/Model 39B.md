## Notes

Primary training seaplane. 100hp Curtiss OXX engine (replaced fire-prone Hall-Scott). Single pontoon with outrigger floats. Convertible to wheeled landing gear. Model 39A had twin floats and Hall-Scott engine (50 built). Model 39B had single pontoon and Curtiss engine (100 built). Deployed to France 1918 for training and reconnaissance.
