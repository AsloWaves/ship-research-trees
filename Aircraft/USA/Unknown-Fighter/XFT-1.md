## Notes

Early low-wing monoplane fighter prototype. Wright R-1510-26 engine (625hp). Enclosed cockpit, sliding canopy. All-metal stressed skin. First flight Jan 16, 1934. Fastest Navy fighter tested at time (235mph) but poor handling. Dangerous spin characteristics, severe tail buffeting. Lost to F2A Buffalo. Only 1 built.
