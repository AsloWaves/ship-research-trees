## Notes

Scout bomber called "Bermuda" by British. Wright R-2600 Cyclone (1700hp). Mid-wing monoplane. "Classic failure" - overweight, underpowered, poor construction, lacked maneuverability. Deemed unsuitable for combat by all operators. None saw combat. Some went directly to scrap heap. 771 built but never used operationally.
