## Notes

Late 1920s carrier fighter. Pratt & Whitney R-1340 Wasp 410hp. First major USN use of radial engines. Served on USS Langley and Lexington (1927-30). Metal leading edges allowed 70-degree dives. Winner of 1926 Curtiss Marine Trophy. Fabric-covered biplane with steel tube fuselage. F6C-1/2/3 had water-cooled inline engines.
