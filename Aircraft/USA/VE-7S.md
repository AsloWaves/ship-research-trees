## Notes

US Navy's first true fighter aircraft. 180hp Wright E-2 engine. Single-seat fighter variant (VE-7S) had front cockpit faired over. First aircraft to launch from US carrier (1922). Good climb rate (5.5 min to 5,000ft). Originally designed as Army trainer (1917). Two-seat trainer variants (VE-7SF, VE-7G, VE-7GF) also used. Historic significance for carrier aviation.
