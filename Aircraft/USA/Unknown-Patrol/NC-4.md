## Notes

First aircraft to cross Atlantic (May 1919, 19 days via Azores). Massive 4-engine flying boat (3 tractor, 1 pusher). 4x 400hp Liberty L-12 engines. Wingspan equivalent to Boeing 707. Built by Curtiss with hull by Herreshoff. Originally designed for WWI anti-submarine patrol. Historic achievement but operationally limited. Only 10 built (NC-1 through NC-10).
