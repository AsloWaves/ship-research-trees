## Notes

Main US Navy anti-submarine patrol aircraft of WWI. Single 360hp Liberty L-12 engine (pusher config). 4.5 hour endurance. Larger wingspan than HS-1L for increased payload. First patrols from France June 1918. 160 deployed to Europe. Made attack on U-boat off Massachusetts July 21, 1918 (unsuccessful). Built by Curtiss, L-W-F, Standard, Gallaudet, Boeing, and Loughead.
