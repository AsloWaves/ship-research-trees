## Notes

Replacement for SBD Dauntless. 1900hp Wright R-2600-20 Cyclone. Nicknamed "Son of a Bitch 2nd Class" due to early reliability issues. Internal bomb bay plus wing racks. Large dive brakes. Combat debut Nov 1943 over Rabaul. Eventually became effective after fixes. Last purpose-built dive bomber. Heavier armament than SBD but more maintenance-intensive. Served through end of WWII.
