## Notes

Twin-engine interceptor for countering B-29. 2x Nakajima Homare 21 engines (1990hp each). Based on J1N1 Gekko night fighter but smaller. First flight Jul 13, 1944. Major disappointment - only 371mph vs specified 414mph. Engines failed to produce promised power, weight increased. 6 completed, 4 lost to accidents. Abandoned Feb 1945.
