## Notes

Kyofu (Mighty Wind). Allied code: Rex. Purpose-designed floatplane interceptor. Nakajima Kasei engine (1460hp). Best-performing floatplane fighter of WWII. Mid-mounted float, auto-retracting stabilizing floats. Basis for land-based N1K1-J Shiden. Only 97 built. Limited service.
