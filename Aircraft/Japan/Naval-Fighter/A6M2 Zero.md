## Notes

Type 0 Carrier Fighter Model 21. Most famous IJN fighter. Nakajima NK1C Sakae engine (950hp). Revolutionary combination of long range, maneuverability, and firepower. Folding wingtips for carrier storage. Internal fuel: 137 US gal + 87 gal drop tank. Dominated early Pacific War with superior maneuverability and range. Lack of armor and self-sealing fuel tanks became liabilities as war progressed. Could carry 2x 60kg bombs. Over 10,000 of all A6M variants produced. Exceptional climb rate and turn performance. Service ceiling 32,810 ft.
