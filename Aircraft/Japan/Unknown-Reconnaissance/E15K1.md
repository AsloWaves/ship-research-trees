## Notes

High-speed reconnaissance floatplane. Mitsubishi MK4S Kasei 24 engine (1850hp). Required to escape land-based fighters with 800nm range. First flight Dec 5, 1941. Excellent performance but production cancelled Feb 1944. Intended for cruiser Oyodo but obsolete by commissioning. Only 15 built (6 prototypes + 9 production).
