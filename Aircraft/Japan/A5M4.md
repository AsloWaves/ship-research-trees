## Notes

Type 96 Carrier Fighter. World's first low-wing monoplane shipboard fighter. Nakajima Kotobuki 41 engine (710hp). Excellent maneuverability and climb rate. Open cockpit. Could carry two 66lb bombs or one 160L drop tank. Predecessor to the famous A6M Zero. Saw extensive combat in China 1937-1941. Gradually replaced by A6M but remained in service through 1945 in training and second-line roles. Inverted gull wings. Retractable landing gear. Fixed-pitch wooden propeller on early variants.
