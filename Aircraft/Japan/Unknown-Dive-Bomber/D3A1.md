## Notes

Type 99 Carrier Bomber. Main IJN dive bomber early Pacific War. Mitsubishi Kinsei 43 engine (1000hp). Fixed landing gear with spats. Hydraulically operated dive brakes. Typical load: 1x 250kg (551lb) centerline bomb + 2x 60kg (132lb) wing bombs. Highly accurate dive bomber. Sank more Allied warships than any other Axis aircraft type. Successful at Pearl Harbor, Indian Ocean raids, Coral Sea. Gradually replaced by D4Y Suisei from 1943. Last biplane-derived dive bomber. D3A2 variant had Kinsei 54 (1300hp) and increased fuel (900L). Total: 478 D3A1, 1016 D3A2.
