## Notes

Type 2 Carrier Reconnaissance / Dive Bomber Suisei (Comet). Fastest IJN carrier bomber. D4Y1/D4Y2 used liquid-cooled Aichi Atsuta engine (licensed DB 601), D4Y3 used Mitsubishi Kinsei 62 radial (1560hp). Retractable landing gear. Internal bomb bay for 1x 500kg (1100lb) bomb. Also served as fast reconnaissance aircraft. Sleek design inspired by Heinkel He 118. Later variants had 13mm rear gun. High performance but complex Atsuta engine caused maintenance issues. Used extensively in Philippines campaign and kamikaze operations late war. Folding wingtips for carrier storage.
