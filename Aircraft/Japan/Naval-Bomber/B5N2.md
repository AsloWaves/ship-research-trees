## Notes

Type 97 Carrier Attack Bomber. Standard IJN carrier-based torpedo bomber through most of WWII. Nakajima Sakae 11 engine (1000hp). Folding wings for carrier storage. Hydraulically operated landing gear retraction. Internal bomb bay for torpedo or bombs. Could carry: 1x 1760lb (800kg) Type 91 torpedo OR 1x 1760lb bomb OR 3x 550lb (250kg) bombs OR 6x 132lb bombs. Played major role at Pearl Harbor, Coral Sea, Midway, Santa Cruz. Reliable and effective but becoming obsolete by 1943. Gradual replacement by B6N Tenzan.
