## Notes

Carrier variant of Ju 87B Stuka dive bomber for Graf Zeppelin. Jumo 211A engine (1100hp). Featured catapult attachment, arresting hook, manually folding outer wing panels, flotation gear, rubber life raft, jettisonable landing gear. Extended range with underwing fuel tanks (800km to 1600km). Preproduction Ju 87C-0 built from 1939, but production Ju 87C-1 never completed as Graf Zeppelin was never finished. Some used operationally as land-based aircraft.
