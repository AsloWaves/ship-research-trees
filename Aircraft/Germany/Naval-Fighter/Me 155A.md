## Notes

Carrier fighter for Graf Zeppelin. DB 605 engine. Bf 109G fuselage with new wings. Folding wings, arrestor gear. Used max Bf 109 parts. Early 1942 Graf Zeppelin cancelled again - Me 155A shelved indefinitely. Later redesigned as BV 155 high-altitude interceptor for B-29 defense (different role). Carrier version never built.
