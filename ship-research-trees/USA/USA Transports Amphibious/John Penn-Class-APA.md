**President Polk Conversion - War Loss** - John Penn-Class was a single attack transport converted from passenger ship. One ship (USS John Penn, APA-23) converted 1941 from SS President Polk. Displacement ~10,500 tons, length 522 feet, carried 1,400 troops. Served in Pacific including Guadalcanal campaign. Sunk 13 August 1943 by Japanese aircraft near Guadalcanal.
