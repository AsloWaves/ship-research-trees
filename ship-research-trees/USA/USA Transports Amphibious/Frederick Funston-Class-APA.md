**C3-S-A1 Attack Transports** - Frederick Funston-Class were WWII attack transports based on the Maritime Commission Type C3-S-A1 hull. Two ships built 1942, served in Pacific and European amphibious operations. Displacement ~12,750 tons, length 492 feet, carried 1,500 troops. Participated in North Africa, Sicily, Normandy, and Pacific campaigns. Both ships decommissioned 1946.

## Ships of the Class

1. USS Frederick Funston (APA-89) - Ex-SS President Jackson
2. USS Calvert (APA-94) - Ex-SS Sea Panther

**Service:** North Africa (1942), Sicily (1943), Normandy (1944), Pacific operations (1944-1945). Both decommissioned 1946.
