**C3-S-A1/A3/A2 Attack Transports** - Windsor-Class were WWII attack transports based on the Maritime Commission Type C3-S-A1/A3/A2 hull. Nine ships built 1943-1944, served in Pacific amphibious operations. Displacement ~12,450 tons, length 459 feet, carried 1,560 troops. Participated in major Pacific landings including Philippines, Iwo Jima, and Okinawa. Post-war service through 1950s-1960s.
