# Starstreak HVM

## Overview
**Nation**: British
**Type**: SAM (MANPADS/SHORAD)
**Year**: 1997

## Specifications
- **Diameter**: 5.1"
- **Length**: 4.6 ft
- **Warhead**: 3.0 lbs (3 tungsten dart submunitions)
- **Speed**: Mach 3.50
- **Range**: 3.8 nm

## System
- **Propulsion**: Two-stage solid rocket (Brambling ejection + Titus main)
- **Guidance**: Laser beam riding (SACLOS)
- **Target**: Aircraft, helicopters, UAVs
- **Platform**: MANPADS, LML tripod, Stormer HVM (8 missiles), RAPIDRanger

## Notes
Fastest MANPADS system in existence (Mach 3.5-4+, over 1,200 m/s). Three tungsten alloy darts separate terminally for increased hit probability. Hit-to-kill system (no proximity fuze). High resistance to IR countermeasures due to laser guidance. Stormer HVM: tracked vehicle with 8 ready + 8-9 stowed missiles. Maximum altitude 16,404 feet (5 km).
