## Special Abilities by Class

**Dreadnought Revolution** (2002): First dreadnought, +30% gun accuracy, UNIQUE
**Super-Dreadnought** (2008): First 13.5" guns, +15% penetration
**Fast Battleship** (2012): Queen Elizabeth-class, +20% speed, +15% range
**Hood Iconic** (2020): +25% prestige, +15% speed, -20% armor (vulnerable magazines)
**Treaty Innovation** (2022): Nelson-class all-forward design, +20% forward firepower
**WWII Ultimate** (2024): King George V-class, +15% AA, +10% reliability
**Last Battleship** (2029): Vanguard, +20% all stats, never saw combat
