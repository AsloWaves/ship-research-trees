## Research Sources

### Primary Research
- NavWeaps.com (technical specifications)
- Wikipedia (historical context)

### Key Findings
1. **H-class Evolution**: Germany planned progressively larger battleships from H-39 (16") to H-44 (20")
2. **Super Yamato**: Japanese A-150 design was nearly complete by 1941, abandoned for carrier production
3. **Nuclear Shells**: W23 made Iowa-class the world's only nuclear-armed battleships
4. **Light AA Proliferation**: Bofors and Oerlikon became universal across all navies by 1942-43
5. **Pre-Dreadnought**: German guns show progression from 24cm (1894) → 28cm (1904) → 30.5cm (1909)
