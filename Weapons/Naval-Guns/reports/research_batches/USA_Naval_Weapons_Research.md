- Mark 1 Twin (Colorado class, 760 tons)

**Ballistic Combinations**: 18 configurations

- Key innovation: Mark 6/7 guns designed to fire 2,700 lb super-heavy Mark 8 AP shell
- Mark 5/8 modernization retained lighter shells only
- Nuclear capability: Mark 23 shell (W23, 15-20kt) for Mark 7 gun only (1953)

- Mark 1: First US 16" naval gun, heavier twin turrets (18" face armor)

- Mark 23: Only nuclear naval gun shell ever deployed by US Navy
