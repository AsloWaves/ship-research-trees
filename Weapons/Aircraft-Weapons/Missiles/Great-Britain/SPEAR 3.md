# SPEAR 3

## Overview
**Nation**: British
**Type**: ASM (Air-to-surface precision strike, anti-ship secondary)
**Year**: 2030 (estimated, in development)

## Specifications
- **Diameter**: 7.1"
- **Length**: 6.6 ft
- **Warhead**: 18 lbs (multi-effect programmable)
- **Speed**: Mach 0.80
- **Range**: 75.0 nm

## System
- **Propulsion**: Pratt & Whitney TJ-150-3 turbojet
- **Guidance**: GPS/INS + multi-mode terminal (MMW radar/SAL/IR)
- **Target**: Ground targets, ships
- **Platform**: F-35B (8 internal), Typhoon (12 external)

## Notes
"Mini cruise missile" with 2-hour turbojet runtime. F-35B carries 8 internally (quad-pack per bay) maintaining stealth. Programmable warhead: tandem shaped-charge (anti-armor), blast/fragmentation (soft targets), breach/penetrate (hardened structures). Two-way datalink for in-flight retargeting and abort. Part of UK FASGW programme. Originally 2025 IOC, re-baselined to early 2030s due to F-35B Block 4 integration.
