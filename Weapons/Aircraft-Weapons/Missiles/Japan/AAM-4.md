# AAM-4 (Type 99 AAM)

## Overview
**Nation**: Japanese
**Type**: AAM (Medium/long-range, active radar)
**Year**: 1999

## Specifications
- **Diameter**: 8.0"
- **Length**: 12.0 ft
- **Warhead**: 35.7 lbs
- **Speed**: Mach 4.50
- **Range**: 46.0 nm (AAM-4), 57.0 nm (AAM-4B)

## System
- **Propulsion**: Solid-fuel rocket motor
- **Guidance**: Data link + active radar homing
- **Target**: Aircraft (beyond-visual-range)
- **Platform**: F-15J, F-2

## Notes
Replacement for AIM-7 Sparrow. AAM-4B (2010) features world's first AESA radar seeker with 21 km detection range. Directional blast-fragmentation warhead with 600 steel penetrating segments. Comparable to AIM-120 AMRAAM.
