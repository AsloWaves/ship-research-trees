# AAM-3 (Type 90 AAM)

## Overview
**Nation**: Japanese
**Type**: AAM (Short-range, infrared-homing)
**Year**: 1991

## Specifications
- **Diameter**: 5.0"
- **Length**: 10.2 ft
- **Warhead**: 10.8 lbs
- **Speed**: Mach 3.00
- **Range**: 7.0 nm

## System
- **Propulsion**: Solid-fuel rocket motor
- **Guidance**: Dual-wavelength IR + UV seeker (all-aspect)
- **Target**: Aircraft
- **Platform**: F-15J, F-2, F-4EJ Kai

## Notes
Replacement for AIM-9L Sidewinder. Advanced canard design for enhanced maneuverability. IRCCM-resistant dual-wavelength seeker with high off-boresight capability. Approximately 400 missiles ordered by JASDF.
