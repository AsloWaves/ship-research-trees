## Notes

OPERATIONAL: Standard FAA airborne torpedo second half WWII. 2,290m @ 40 knots. 388 lbs TNT (carrier), 544 lbs Torpex (land-based). Drop speed up to 275 mph, max drop altitude 400 ft. Higher air pressure burner than Mk XII. Used by Barracuda, Firefly, Beaufighter. Improved performance over Mk XII.
