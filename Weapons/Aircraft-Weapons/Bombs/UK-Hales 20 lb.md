## Notes

RETIRED: WWI first-generation aircraft bomb. Invented by Martin Hale. Steel pear-shaped body, 4.5 lbs amatol. 127mm diameter, 585mm length. Used extensively by RNAS early war, then RFC from 1915. Manufactured at Faversham, Kent. Obsolete by 1918. Very primitive accuracy.
