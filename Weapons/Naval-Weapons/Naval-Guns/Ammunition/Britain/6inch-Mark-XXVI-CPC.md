## Notes

Semi-armor-piercing shell for Town-class and Colony-class light cruisers with 6"/50 BL Mark XXIII guns. The CPC (Capped Piercing Common) designation indicated a shell designed to be effective against both armored and unarmored targets. Fired at 2,758 fps muzzle velocity. Used extensively in Mediterranean and Atlantic operations, including the Battles of Cape Matapan and North Cape. Featured improved ballistics compared to earlier 6-inch shells.
