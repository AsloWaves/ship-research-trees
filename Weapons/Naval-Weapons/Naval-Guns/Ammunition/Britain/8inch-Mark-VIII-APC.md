## Notes

Standard APC shell for County-class heavy cruisers equipped with the 8"/50 Mark VIII gun. Fired at 2,805 fps muzzle velocity. Used by vessels including HMS Norfolk, HMS Dorsetshire, and HMS Exeter (which engaged Admiral Graf Spee at the Battle of the River Plate). The shell featured a 4crh design typical of British heavy cruiser ammunition of the Washington Treaty era, optimized for engaging enemy cruisers at medium to long range.
