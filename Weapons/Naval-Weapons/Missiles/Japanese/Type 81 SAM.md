**Nation**: Japanese
**Type**: SAM (Short-range)
**Year**: 1981

## Specifications
- **Diameter**: 6.3"
- **Length**: 8.9 ft
- **Warhead**: 20.3 lbs
- **Speed**: Mach 2.40
- **Range**: 5.4 nm (7.6 nm Type 81C)

## System
- **Propulsion**: Solid-fuel rocket motor
- **Guidance**: Inertial + infrared terminal homing
- **Target**: Low-flying aircraft, helicopters, cruise missiles
- **Platform**: Ground-based, mobile (Isuzu 6Ã—6 truck)

## Notes
Fills gap between Stinger (MANPADS) and Hawk (medium-range). Fire unit: 1 fire control vehicle (3D phased array radar, 16.2 nm range) + 2 launcher vehicles (4 missiles each). Type 81C upgrade adds active radar seeker and extended 14 km range. 93 fire units deployed across JGSDF/JASDF/JMSDF.
