## Notes

First prototype of A6M replacement. Nakajima Homare 22 engine (2000hp). First flight May 6, 1944 - 4 years after development start. Excellent handling/maneuverability but severely underpowered. Top speed only 356mph, similar to A6M5 Zero. Navy ordered development stop Jul 30, 1944. Only 3 A7M1 built. Later A7M2 with Ha-43 engine more successful.
