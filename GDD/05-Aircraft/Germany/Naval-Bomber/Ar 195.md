## Notes

Carrier-based torpedo bomber for Graf Zeppelin. BMW 132 engine (880hp). Biplane derivative of Ar 95. Arrestor hook, catapult equipment, tall canopy. Three prototypes flown 1937. Suffered excess drag, poor flyability. Rejected 1938 in favor of Fi 167 which was superior. Only 3 built.
