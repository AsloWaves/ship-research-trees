## Notes

Legendary multi-role fighter! 2x J79-GE-8 turbojets, 16950lbf afterburner each. Vietnam War workhorse. Originally no gun (missiles only). Mach 2.2 capable. Served USN, USAF, USMC. Tandem seating. Large payload capacity. Smoke trail from engines. Set multiple records. Nicknamed "Double Ugly" and "Rhino". Extremely versatile - fighter, bomber, recon, Wild Weasel.
