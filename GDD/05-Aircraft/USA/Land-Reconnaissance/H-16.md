## Notes

Large twin-engine patrol flying boat. Primary anti-submarine aircraft of WWI. 2x 400hp Liberty engines. Used at NAF Killingholme (UK), Brest & St. Trojan (France), Queenstown (Ireland). First USN aircraft with twin engines. Radio equipped. Built by both Curtiss and Naval Aircraft Factory.
