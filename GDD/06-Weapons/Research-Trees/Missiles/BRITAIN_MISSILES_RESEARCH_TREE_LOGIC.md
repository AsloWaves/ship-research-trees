## Research Prerequisites Logic

### SAM Systems Evolution
1. **First Generation** (1961-1973)
   - Sea Slug Mk 1 (2200): Heavy beam-riding SAM → Mk 2/3 improvements
   - Bloodhound (2230): Land-based SAM adapted for naval use
   - Convergence at Sea Dart (2210)

2. **Second Generation** (1973-2009)
   - Sea Dart (2210): Area defense → 4 modification blocks
   - Sea Wolf (2220): Point defense VLS → CAMM evolution

3. **Modern Generation** (2009-2025)
   - Sea Viper/Aster (2214): Franco-British collaboration
   - CAMM/Sea Ceptor (2223): Common Anti-Air Modular Missile
   - CAMM-ER extended range variant

### SSM Systems Evolution
1. **Anti-Ship Era** (1975-2008)
   - Exocet MM38/40 progression (French collaboration)
   - Sea Skua helicopter-launched lightweight

2. **Modern Strike** (2008-2025)
   - Storm Shadow cruise missiles
   - Tomahawk UK variants
   - Sea Venom/Martlet next-generation

### ASW Systems Evolution
1. **Helicopter-Launched** (1968-1989)
   - Ikara long-range ASW → Mod 1/2 improvements
   - Stingray lightweight torpedo integration

2. **Modern ASW** (2005-2020)
   - Sea Urchin successor systems
   - Sea Spear multi-role ASW

## Nation Progression Logic

**Starting Technologies** (1961):
- Sea Slug Mk 1 (Node 2200): First British SAM → unlocks Sea Dart
- Sea Wolf GWS.25 (Node 2220): Point defense → unlocks CAMM
- Bloodhound Mk I (Node 2230): Early SAM development
- MM38 Exocet (Node 2240): Anti-ship capability → unlocks Sea Skua/Storm Shadow
- Ikara GWS.40 (Node 2260): ASW capability → unlocks modern systems

**Technology Convergence Points**:
- Node 2210 (Sea Dart): Unifies Sea Slug and Bloodhound lines
- Node 2223 (CAMM): Replaces Sea Wolf with modular system
- Node 2243 (Exocet Block 3): Enables Storm Shadow and Tomahawk branches

**High-Cost Nodes** (Research Cost >8000):
- Sea Viper Aster 30 (8500): Advanced area defense
- Sea Viper Block 1NT (9500): Network-enabled defense
- Tomahawk Block IV UK (10000): Long-range strike
- Tomahawk Block V UK (11500): Maritime strike network

**Tree Depth**: 11 generations (1961-2025, average 5.8 years/generation)

**Anglo-American Interoperability**:
- Tomahawk variants (Nodes 2251-2252): US technology transfer
- CAMM compatibility with ESSM launchers
- Sea Viper shares technology with US SM family
