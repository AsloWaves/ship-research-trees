# RIM-161A SM-3 Block IA (JMSDF)

## Overview
**Nation**: Japanese
**Type**: SAM (Ballistic Missile Defense)
**Year**: 2007

## Specifications
- **Diameter**: 13.5" (body) / 21" (booster)
- **Length**: 21.5 ft
- **Warhead**: Kinetic Kill Vehicle (0 lbs explosive)
- **Speed**: Mach 8.00
- **Range**: 380.0 nm (exoatmospheric)

## System
- **Propulsion**: Three-stage solid fuel + KKV thrusters
- **Guidance**: Inertial + GPS + IR seeker on KKV
- **Target**: Short and intermediate-range ballistic missiles
- **Platform**: Kongō-class, Atago-class destroyers (Aegis/Mk 41 VLS)

## Notes
First successful test December 2007 from JS Kongō. Intercepts ballistic missiles at 500 km altitude (exoatmospheric). US-Japan Joint Cooperative Research (1999-2007) involved Japanese development of sensors, kinetic warheads, propulsion, and nosecones. SM-3 Block IIA is joint Raytheon/Mitsubishi Heavy Industries project.
