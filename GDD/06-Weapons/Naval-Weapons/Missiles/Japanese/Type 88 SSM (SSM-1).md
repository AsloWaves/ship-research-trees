# Type 88 SSM (SSM-1)

## Overview
**Nation**: Japanese
**Type**: SSM (Ground-based coastal defense)
**Year**: 1988

## Specifications
- **Diameter**: 13.8"
- **Length**: 16.4 ft
- **Warhead**: 496 lbs
- **Speed**: Mach 0.90
- **Range**: 97.0 nm

## System
- **Propulsion**: TJM3 turbojet + solid rocket booster
- **Guidance**: Inertial (INS) + active radar terminal homing
- **Target**: Surface ships
- **Platform**: Truck-mounted TEL (6 missiles per launcher)

## Notes
Japan's first ground-based anti-ship missile for coastal defense. Derived from ASM-1 (Type 80) air-launched missile. Sea-skimming flight profile with radar-absorbing coating. Home-on-Jam capability. Battery: 4 launchers (24 missiles), fire control center, radar, 4 reload vehicles. 45-minute deployment time. Succeeded by Type 12 SSM with GPS guidance and extended range.
