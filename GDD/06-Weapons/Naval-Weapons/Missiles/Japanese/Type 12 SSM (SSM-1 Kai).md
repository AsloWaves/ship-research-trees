# Type 12 SSM (SSM-1 Kai)

## Overview
**Nation**: Japanese
**Type**: SSM (Ground-based coastal defense)
**Year**: 2012

## Specifications
- **Diameter**: 13.8"
- **Length**: 16.4 ft
- **Warhead**: 496 lbs
- **Speed**: Mach 0.90
- **Range**: 108.0 nm (Block 0), 486.0 nm (Block 1), 540.0 nm (Block 2)

## System
- **Propulsion**: Mitsubishi TJM3 turbojet + solid rocket booster
- **Guidance**: INS/GPS + active radar terminal + TERCOM
- **Target**: Surface ships
- **Platform**: Truck-mounted TEL (6 missiles per launcher)

## Notes
Improved successor to Type 88 SSM-1. GPS mid-course guidance and enhanced TERCOM (terrain-following). Network-enabled targeting from other platforms. Can fire from concealed positions (valleys, forests). Sea-skimming at 5-6 meters in terminal phase. Block 1/2 variants dramatically extend range to 900-1,000+ km. Basis for Type 17 ship-launched variant.
