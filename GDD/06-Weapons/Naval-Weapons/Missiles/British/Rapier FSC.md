# Rapier FSC (Field Standard C)

## Overview
**Nation**: British
**Type**: SAM (Short-range)
**Year**: 1997

## Specifications
- **Diameter**: 5.2"
- **Length**: 7.3 ft
- **Warhead**: 3.1 lbs
- **Speed**: Mach 2.50
- **Range**: 4.3 nm

## System
- **Propulsion**: Two-stage solid rocket (boost-sustain)
- **Guidance**: SACLOS radio command + Dagger/Blindfire radar
- **Target**: Low-level aircraft, helicopters, UAVs
- **Platform**: Towed launcher or M548 tracked carrier (8 missiles)

## Notes
Most advanced Rapier configuration with digital autopilot. Dagger surveillance radar (15 km, 75+ threats/second), Blindfire tracking radar (15 km, all-weather). 6-second response time, dual-target engagement capability. Combat-proven (Falklands 1982). Over 25,000 missiles produced. Exported to Singapore (JERNAS), Iran, Turkey, UAE, Indonesia, Switzerland.
