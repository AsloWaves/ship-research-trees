## Notes

The 20.3cm Spgr. L/4.7 was the high-explosive shell for Admiral Hipper-class heavy cruisers. This shell was used against unarmored and lightly armored targets, as well as for shore bombardment. The shell featured a base-detonating fuse and provided good fragmentation. Used extensively during various operations including convoy attacks in the Arctic and Atlantic. Admiral Hipper used these shells during attacks on Allied convoys, including actions in the Barents Sea.
