## Notes

High-explosive shell for the QF 4-inch Mark XVI dual-purpose gun, one of the most successful British AA guns of WWII. Used fixed QF ammunition for rapid fire. Fired at 2,660 fps muzzle velocity with a rate of fire up to 20 rounds per minute. Equipped on numerous cruisers, destroyers, and escort vessels as secondary AA armament. Featured time fuzes for AA work and impact fuzes for surface targets. The Mark XVI gun/shell combination was highly regarded for its reliability and effectiveness against aircraft.
