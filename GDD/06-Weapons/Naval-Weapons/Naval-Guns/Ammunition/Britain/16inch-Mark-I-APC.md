## Notes

APC shell designed for the Nelson-class battleships (HMS Nelson and HMS Rodney). Featured 4crh ogive with ballistic cap. Fired at 2,614 fps muzzle velocity from the unique 45-caliber forward-mounted triple turrets. The shell was designed under Washington Naval Treaty weight limitations. Notable for use at the Battle of the Denmark Strait and in the hunt for the Bismarck.
