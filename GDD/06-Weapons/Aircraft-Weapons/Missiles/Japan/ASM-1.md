# ASM-1 (Type 80 ASM)

## Overview
**Nation**: Japanese
**Type**: ASM (Air-launched anti-ship)
**Year**: 1980

## Specifications
- **Diameter**: 13.2"
- **Length**: 13.0 ft
- **Warhead**: 315 lbs
- **Speed**: Mach 0.90
- **Range**: 27.0 nm

## System
- **Propulsion**: Solid-fuel rocket motor
- **Guidance**: Inertial + active radar terminal homing
- **Target**: Surface ships
- **Platform**: F-1, F-2, P-3C, F-4EJ Kai

## Notes
Japan's first indigenously-developed anti-ship missile. Sea-skimming flight profile. Developed by Mitsubishi Heavy Industries. Replaced by longer-range ASM-2 (170 km) starting 1995, then supersonic ASM-3 (2018).
