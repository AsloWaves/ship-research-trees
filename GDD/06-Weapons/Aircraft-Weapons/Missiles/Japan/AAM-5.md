# AAM-5 (Type 04 AAM)

## Overview
**Nation**: Japanese
**Type**: AAM (Short-range, imaging infrared)
**Year**: 2004

## Specifications
- **Diameter**: 5.1"
- **Length**: 10.2 ft
- **Warhead**: 15.0 lbs (estimated)
- **Speed**: Mach 3.00
- **Range**: 18.9 nm

## System
- **Propulsion**: Solid-fuel rocket with thrust vectoring
- **Guidance**: Imaging infrared seeker (IIR) + inertial
- **Target**: Aircraft, helicopters
- **Platform**: F-15J, F-2, F-35A

## Notes
4th generation short-range missile with LOAL (Lock-On After Launch) capability. Thrust vector control for enhanced agility. AAM-5B (2016) adds dual-wavelength IR seeker and enhanced flare resistance. Comparable to AIM-9X Block II. Helmet-mounted cueing system compatible.
