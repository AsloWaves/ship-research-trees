# ASM-3 (XASM-3)

## Overview
**Nation**: Japanese
**Type**: ASM (Air-launched anti-ship, supersonic)
**Year**: 2018

## Specifications
- **Diameter**: 13.8"
- **Length**: 19.7 ft
- **Warhead**: Classified (heavy warhead)
- **Speed**: Mach 3.00
- **Range**: 81.0 nm (ASM-3), 162.0 nm (ASM-3A)

## System
- **Propulsion**: Integral rocket ramjet (IRR)
- **Guidance**: INS/GPS + active/passive radar composite
- **Target**: Surface ships
- **Platform**: F-2 (up to 2 missiles)

## Notes
First Western anti-ship missile achieving high supersonic speed with heavy warhead. Development completed 2018, ASM-3A extended-range variant entered service 2021. Sea-skimming terminal phase. Fixed-geometry supersonic air intakes. Replaces ASM-1/ASM-2. Part of Japan's three-pronged anti-ship strategy (JSM stealth, LRASM range, ASM-3 speed).
