# JNAAM (Joint New AAM)

## Overview
**Nation**: Japanese (joint with UK)
**Type**: AAM (Medium-range, beyond-visual-range)
**Year**: 2024 (research concluded, cancelled)

## Specifications
- **Diameter**: 7.0"
- **Length**: 12.2 ft
- **Warhead**: 55 lbs
- **Speed**: Mach 4.00
- **Range**: 110.0 nm

## System
- **Propulsion**: Throttleable ducted rocket (ramjet)
- **Guidance**: Ka-band GaN AESA radar (AAM-4B seeker)
- **Target**: Aircraft (BVR engagement)
- **Platform**: F-35A, F-35B (intended, not deployed)

## Notes
Japan-UK collaborative program combining MBDA Meteor airframe with Japanese AAM-4B AESA seeker. World's first Ka-band AESA AAM seeker. Successfully tested April 2023. Cancelled March 2024 due to French concerns over Meteor technology transfer. Research informing Japan's next domestic AAM for GCAP fighter. Never entered production.
