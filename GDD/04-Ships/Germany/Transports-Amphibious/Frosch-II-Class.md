**East German Support Ships** - Frosch II-class were logistic support ships of East German Volksmarine. 2 built 1976-1993, development of Frosch I.

**Key Specs:** ~2,000 tons | ~100m | 2Ã— diesel engines | Logistic support configuration

**Innovation:** Development of Frosch I (Project 108) LST design into logistic support variant (Project 109). Built VEB Peenewerft, Wolgast. Two ships constructed. Similar hull to Frosch I but configured for supply/support role rather than tank landing.

**Design:** Based on Frosch I hull. Diesel propulsion. Modified superstructure and cargo holds for supply mission. Details limited in available sources.

**Service:** Volksmarine logistic support 1976-1993. After German reunification, not integrated into Bundesmarine. Both ships transferred to Indonesia 1993 along with Frosch I class.
