## Historical Notes

Modern integrated bridges emerged from the Aegis Combat System and similar programs. The emphasis shifted from raw sensor capability to information processing and decision support. A modern destroyer's bridge crew is smaller than a WWII corvette's, yet handles vastly more complex operations.
