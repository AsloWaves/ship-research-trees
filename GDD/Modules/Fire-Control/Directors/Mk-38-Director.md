**Battleship Eyes** - The Mk 38 was the USN's main battery director for fast battleships. Massive 18-ton armored housing with 8m stereoscopic rangefinder. Paired with Mk 8 fire control radar for blind fire capability. Equipped Iowa, South Dakota, North Carolina classes.

**Key Specs:** 18,100 kg | 8m rangefinder | -5° to +45° | 10°/sec train | 3-axis gyro | 7 crew

**Features:**
- Fully armored: 37mm splinter protection for vital optical equipment
- Mk 8/13 radar: Microwave FC radar for surface ranging in any weather
- 3-axis stabilization: Maintains accuracy in heavy seas
- Stereoscopic ranging: Faster than coincidence at long range

**Limitations:** Heavy (18 tons). Surface only - no AA capability. Slower tracking than Mk 37.

**Combat Record:** Guadalcanal (1942) - radar-directed night fire. Philippine Sea (1944) - long-range surface engagement. Surigao Strait (1944) - radar fire in darkness devastated Japanese force.

**Ships:** Iowa-class (2), South Dakota-class (2), North Carolina-class (2). All USN fast battleships.
