## National Characteristics

**USA**: Best radar integration, excellent dual-purpose (Mk 37 GFCS). First effective radar FC. Industrial standardization.

**UK**: Evolved from WWI Dreyer Table. Separate surface/AA doctrine. Good radar integration from 1941.

**Japan**: Optical excellence - 15m rangefinders, night fighting focus. Fatal radar integration lag.

**Germany**: Precision stereoscopic optics, excellent mechanics. Catastrophic failure to integrate radar with FC.

## Era Progression
| Era | Technology | Accuracy | Range |
|-----|------------|----------|-------|
| 1906-1918 | Rangefinders + calculators | ~3% at 10km | 10-15km |
| 1918-1935 | Mechanical computers | ~2% at 15km | 15-25km |
| 1935-1945 | Integrated systems + radar | ~1-2% at 20km | 20-35km |
