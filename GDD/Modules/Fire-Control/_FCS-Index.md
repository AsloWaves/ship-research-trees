## National Characteristics

**USA**: Best radar integration, excellent dual-purpose (Mk 37 GFCS). First effective radar FC.
**UK**: Evolved from WWI, separate surface/AA systems. Dreyer Table pioneered mechanical FC.
**Japan**: Optical excellence, 15m rangefinders, night fighting focus. Lagged in radar.
**Germany**: Precision stereoscopic optics, good mechanical computers. Poor radar integration.

## Era Progression
| Era | Technology | Accuracy | Range |
|-----|------------|----------|-------|
| 1906-1918 | Rangefinders + calculators | ~3% at 10km | 10-15km |
| 1918-1935 | Mechanical computers | ~2% at 15km | 15-25km |
| 1935-1945 | Integrated systems + radar | ~1-2% at 20km | 20-35km |
